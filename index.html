Top Findings

  • Instagram leads market share at 30%, followed by YouTube at 25%.
  • 25-34 age group is the most active demographic on social media.
  • Average user spends 73 minutes per day on social platforms.
  • Snapchat users have highest daily usage (80 min/day on average).
  • Instagram has highest engagement rate at 2.1% average.
